FAQs

Is BumpBudget free?

Yes. This version is built as an academic project and is free to use.

How does forecasting work?

Forecasting is based on your profile details (due date, income, leave dates) and typical cost patterns. Predictions are guidance, not financial advice.

Can I change my details later?

Yes. You can update your profile at any time to refresh your forecasts.
